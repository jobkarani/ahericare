Home Nursing Services

Care Giver Services

Home care services offered by certified nurses and nurses aids. Assisted living care for the elderly and disabled persons:

  • Grooming needs
  • Toileting
  • Ambulation
  • Medication reminders
  • Companionship
  • Pressure area care
  • Feeding the patient
  • Running errands for the patient
  • Accompanying patient to the hospital

Skilled Nursing

Professional Home Nursing Services offered by licenced Nurses:

  • Injections
  • Drug administration: Intravenous, intramuscular, subcutaneous injection and Per oral
  • Wound care
  • Suctioning
  • Feeding through NG tube and the PEG
  • Pressure area care
  • Oxygen administration
  • Vitals observation
  • Assistance with ambulation
  • Companionship

Wound Care, Colostomy Care

  • Colostomy and Catheter care
  • Trauma and Burn Wounds
  • Post-surgical wound care
  • Pressure Area Care and Sore dressing
  • Regular wound cleaning and dressing

Physiotherapy Services

  • Musculoskeletal physiotherapy
  • Neurological physiotherapy
  • Paediatric physiotherapy
  • Rehabilitation and Pain Management
  • Sport physiotherapy

Nutrition Counselling

  • Assisted Feeding
  • Nasogastric Tube Feeding (NGT)
  • Percutaneous endoscopic gastrostomy tube feeding
  • Total Parenteral Feeding

Ambulance Services

For swift and immediate patient transfers, we provide emergency and non-emergency transit. This enables early reach and access to advanced medical and hospital care. For patients in need of regular hospital visits; dialysis, chemotherapy we have a non-emergency option and they have are always accompained their home nurse to allow continuity of care.
